# Ollie Gives, Ollie Takes

## High concept
A browser-playable tactical card roguelite inspired by Triple Triad and Slay the Spire.

## Core fantasy
Place spirit-creature cards on a 3x3 board, destroy enemy cards through directional power comparisons, manage draw/discard cycles, and build a stronger run over time.

## Pillars
- readable tactical placement
- strong board tension on a small grid
- discard / recycle loop
- roguelite run structure
- soft, magical, nature-inspired mood
- prototype-first development

## Visual direction
- inspired by Ori-style mood and readability
- soft fantasy forest atmosphere
- luminous, clean, emotional
- placeholder visuals are acceptable for prototype
- gameplay readability is more important than final art quality

## V1 scope
- one battle screen
- human vs AI
- same starter deck on both sides
- no card powers yet
- no account system yet
- no monetization
- no backend required for V1

## Design priority
Prove that the core battle loop is fun before building content-heavy systems.
